Email Writing Best Practices Checklist:

Write a clear and meaningful subject line
Begin with a respectful greeting (e.g., Dear Sir/Madam)
Use formal and professional language throughout the email
Keep the message concise and focused
Avoid using slang, abbreviations, or casual expressions
Ensure correct grammar, spelling, and punctuation
State the purpose of the email clearly
Close the email politely (e.g., Thank you / Yours sincerely)
Add your name and relevant contact details at the end
Review and proofread the email before sending
