During this task, I learned the importance of professional communication in academic and professional environments. Poor digital communication can create misunderstandings and negatively impact opportunities. For example, if a student sends an email to a professor without a subject line, greeting, or proper explanation, the professor may ignore the message or misunderstand the request. Similarly, using informal language in professional emails can create a negative impression.

To avoid such situations, it is important to write clear subject lines, use professional greetings, and maintain polite language. This task helped me understand how structured communication improves clarity and professionalism. I also learned the importance of responsible social media usage, as online behavior can influence academic and career opportunities.
