Tips to Prevent Cybercrime:

Avoid clicking on unfamiliar or suspicious links
Always confirm the authenticity of the sender’s email address
Do not share passwords, OTPs, or personal information with anyone
Create strong and different passwords for your accounts
Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
Regularly update your software and antivirus programs
Carefully check website URLs before providing any information
Do not perform sensitive transactions on public Wi-Fi networks
Stay informed about common cyber threats such as phishing
