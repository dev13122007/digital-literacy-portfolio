During this task, I learned how common phishing attacks have become and how easily people can fall victim to cybercrime. I was surprised to see that attackers often create very realistic emails and websites that look identical to official platforms. Many users click on suspicious links without verifying them, which leads to financial loss and misuse of personal data.

This research helped me understand the importance of being cautious while using digital platforms. One habit I will personally change is checking website URLs carefully before entering any personal or financial information. I will also avoid clicking on unknown links and will enable two-factor authentication for my important accounts. This task increased my awareness about online safety and responsible digital behavior.
